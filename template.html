ABOUT US.

Our story: We're a sincere company with a straightforward vision. We believe that there is an amazing amount to be learned by working in a "real-world" web production environment, alongside other like minded-teens. With this in mind, we began to gather people together in May of 2022. From there, the group quickly expanded to five members, and we decided upon the name o fthe company is "Premier Sdn Bhd". What we bring to the table: Our volunteers cover a wide range of duties from sorting donations to ensure a consistent supply of stock, to uploading books on the website to provide our customers with a great selection of genres, and picking and dispatching orders to ensure fast and speedy delivery. Depending on the needs of the store, and your interest, you might be involved in the following: -Sorting, selectingand cleaning donated boooks to ensure sufficient stock is listed daily on the website. -Using the Premier Bookstore guidline t ensure all books are sold at the best possible price. -Uploading books on the website and creating short, appealing, and accurate blurbs to help our customers with their book selection. -Shelving books into appropriate categories. Picking and packing orders to ensure fast and speedy delivery.
